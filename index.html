Milestones

LWS Construction Inc. provides specialized building services to a range of clients throughout the New York metropolitan area.  LWS Construction Inc. is not only adept at creating beautiful spaces, but is also experienced at systematically and efficiently maintaining them.  Based in Manhattan, NY, LWS Construction Inc. operated business committed to elevating industry standards for building services.  LWS Construction Inc. specializes in high end commercial and residential projects, and maintains a strong track record in lobby, hallway, and office renovation.  LWS Construction Inc. provides comprehensive janitorial and building maintenance services which includes mechanical engineering, and it is responsible for maintaining a number of landmark properties in the tri-state area.

Team

Chuang Liu

Founder & CEO

Chuang Liu founded both the headquarter company and the US subsidiary. He has 23 years of project management and construction experiences in the construction and decoration industry. After graduating from Harbin Engineering University with a master's degree in civil engineering, he joined China Construction Corporation, China's largest state-owned construction company, in 1996 and worked for nearly 10 years. He has been an expert covering all processes from design to construction completion. He has served as the chief executive and chief engineer of many large projects such as the construction project of Bayer Pharmaceuticals Beijing factory, Xinhua Life's headquarters building, Incredible Building, and Scientific Research Building of Chinese Academy of Sciences project. Mr. Chuang Liu founded his own construction company in Beijing in 2005 based on the core values of teamwork, integrity and commitment. He holds the belief to provide valuable services to clients and build partnerships in the community. His insistence on quality of service, hard work, and responsiveness to client needs build up the company credo and has led to its success in a competitive industry. The company’s ability to address changing conditions and demands, find and serve diverse markets has resulted in a rapid expansion of the company. In the following ten years, he completed numerous projects such as the Phase I to Phase III new construction projects in Huairou Park of the Chinese Academy of Sciences with a total construction area of nearly 100,000 square meters; The new office building project of Beijing Medical Device Inspection Center with a total construction area of nearly 20,000 square meters ; The comprehensive renovation project of Kexu Community of the Chinese Academy of Sciences with a total construction area of nearly 40,000 square meters, as well as several upgrading and renovation projects such as the construction project of Shangri-La Park in beijing happy valley. Mr. Chuang Liu’s professional level and many years of practical project experiences have won him a good reputation in the construction industry. Mr. Liu Chuang has the qualification of a professional first-class construction engineer in China and the qualification of a member of the China Bid Evaluation Expert Committee.

History

LWS Construction Inc. (formerly known as "Beijing XinYe ZhiHui Construction Engineering Co., Ltd.") was founded in 2005. Upholding the persistent commitment of servicing our client with honor, dignity and respect as well as delivering construction projects with high quality and punctuality, the company has been growing immensely with the staff of more than 50 executive project managers, engineers as well as individual project valuing from US$1.5 million to US$21 million. Our greatest reward is the recognition in the industry and repeat businesses that we receive from our clients. The company has long cooperated with Beijing Urban Construction Group, Beijing Construction and Engineering Group, Beijing Shouhua Construction and Management Co., Ltd., Beijing Capital Development Holding (Group) Co., Ltd. and etc. We completed a series of national and Beijing key projects with high quality and efficiency, including the new construction of Beijing Medical Apparatus and Instruments Inspection Office, the new construction of Institute of Electronics, Chinese Academy of Sciences Huairou Park, the renovation of Chinese Academy of Sciences Kexu Community, the renovation of parking lots in government offices parks of Chinese Academy of Sciences, the new construction of teaching buildings of Capital Normal University，the renovation of office buildings in National Geographic Industry Park, the renovation and new construction of Beijing Confucius Temple and imperial academy Shisanjing Forest of Steles and won the gold medal of " Great Wall Cup" in Beijing's structure and the national award for quality engineering for many times. Major projects currently under construction include the renovation of the National Museum, the laboratory renovation of the Institute of Physics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the new construction of Beijing Happy Valley Shangri-La. The founder Liu Chuang came to the United States in 2014 and established LWS Construction Inc. During a short period of five years, the company completed/undertook nearly 20 projects. Representative projects include: 247 east 28th rental building renovation and maintenance; 277 Fifth Ave luxury building renovation; 224 West 30th street office building renovation, and etc. In the future development of our company, we will make our company a leading comprehensive service provider for urban construction in China and the United States by implementing the strategy of " strengthening our headquarters, increasing our capital, promoting the coordinated development of the industrial chain", and strive to realize the vision of "strong enterprise and happy employees".
